Flow :

The game site layout:

Our game will consist of four pages. The first page will be used for registration before entering the game. The second one will be dedicated for the rules of the game. Then, the following one will showcase the game, while the last page will display the results of other players as well as our own. From the results page, players will have the option to replay or return to the registration page.



Home page :

Before starting to play, the player's nickname must be entered. If the user selects a nickname that has been used before, they will be redirected to the home page to choose an unused one.



Level 1 :

The player initiates the game, which enters a loop terminating when the astronaut's three lives are depleted. Within this loop, representing level 1, the astronaut must evade birds; failure results in the loss of a life. If he jump on a cloud, he do a smaller leap. When the astronaut successfully navigate throught the loop with sufficient lives, he progresses to the next level, denoted as "Level 2" on the diagram.



Level 2 :

In level 2 of the game, the player enters a loop similar to that of level 1. The collision with satellites results in the astronaut losing a life, while avoiding such collisions allows the game to continue. If he colides with a rocket, the astronaut is propulsed 5 stages ahead in the game. Encountering space debris disorients the astronaut, causing the left and right controls to be inverted for 5 seconds. When the astronaut successfully navigate throught the loop with sufficient lives, he progresses to the next level, denoted as "Level 3" on the diagram.



Level 3 :

During level 3, the player enters a game loop. If the astronaut collides with meteors or gets drawn into a black hole, they lose a life. Contact with aliens sends the astronaut back to the beginning of level 3. Successfully navigating through the obstacles allows the astronaut to reach the moon, representing the conclusion and victory in the game.
