The astronaut

The astronaut is the character of our game. He will have to complete the mission to the moon !

Galactic dangers

Reach the moon by navigating through obstacles and jumping higher and higher. Your success will depend on your agility and ability to anticipate dangers coming from all directions. Your mission culminates in a triumphant landing on the lunar surface. Only by overcoming the obstacles that lie in your path can you achieve this monumental feat

During one game, the astronaut has 3 lifes represented by the following heart.

Level 1 : Leaving Earth

Colliding with birds results in the loss of a life.

Platforms represented by clouds have smaller boundaries, making them trickier to land on.

Level 2 : Reaching Lunar Orbit

Colliding with satellites results in the loss of a life.

The astronaut experiences temporary (5 seconds) disorientation when he collides whith special debries. When clicking right he will then move left, and vice versa. During this time, the character flashes.

Level 3 : Reaching Interplanetary Space

Colliding with meteorites results in the loss of a life.

Being drawn into a black hole leads in the loss of a life.

Contact with aliens results in the astronaut being kidnapped and sent back to the beginning of the third level.

The transition between the level will be a cloud
