Galactic Jumper

By Nicole Paseri, Kevin Ferreira and Madeline da Silva Dias

Game Description

Embark on an intergalactic adventure as you take on the role of a courageous astronaut on a quest for the moon!
Your mission is to skillfully jump across stars and planets while avoiding formidable obstacles such as birds and planes in the initial levels, and later on, satellites and meteorites that will cross your path.


Ultimate Objective:

Reach the moon by navigating through obstacles and jumping higher and higher. Your success will depend on your agility and ability to anticipate dangers coming from all directions.


Cosmic Challenges:

With each stage of your cosmic journey, you'll face new challenges and traverse increasingly treacherous terrain. Begin your odyssey as the astronaut launches from Earth's surface, encountering hazards such as birds and clouds that threaten to impede your progress. As you ascend to the second level and venture into interplanetary space, beware of satellites and erratic particle shifts that could send you off course. However, fear not, for helpful boosts like rockets will aid your advancement. Reach the third level and navigate the perilous lunar orbit, dodging meteorites, evading black holes, and outmaneuvering curious aliens. Your ultimate destination awaits as you strive to land safely on the moon, completing your extraordinary journey through the cosmos.


Level Up:

With each series of successful jumps, you will advance to higher levels. Each level will introduce new challenges and more distant stars, bringing you closer to your lunar destination.


Breathtaking Graphics:

Explore a visually captivating universe with sparkling stars, colorful planets, and detailed obstacles. The visual experience will match the excitement of your interplanetary journey.


Inspiration:

This game draws inspiration from space exploration and the conquest of the moon, with gameplay reminiscent of the classic joy of arcade games while providing a modern and immersive adventure.


Academic Development:

This game project is undertaken as part of the game development module within the computer science program, applying the skills acquired in the academic setting.
