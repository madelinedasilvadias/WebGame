By Nicole Paseri, Kevin Ferreira and Madeline da Silva Dias

Home Page

On this page, players will be prompted to enter their nickname, which will be used throughout the game and featured in the Wall of Fame at the end. Additionally, they will have the option to review the game rules if they are unclear before starting the game.



Rules

The rules page serves as an essential resource for players, providing a complete immersion into the game's universe. In addition to presenting the game description and the number of lives allocated per session, this page provides detailed instructions on how to play, including controls, possible actions, and any other relevant aspects of gameplay. Players will also find helpful tips to enhance their performance and master encountered challenges. By consolidating all these essential pieces of information in one place, the rules page aims to offer players a seamless gaming experience.



Game

In the square below, players will have the opportunity to immerse themselves in the game's universe and hope to win.



Wall of fame

When the game is finished, the wall of fame will be displayed. It will show the player's position in the ranking, as well as that of the top players who have previously achieved scores based on the number of kilometers the astronaut successfully traveled away from Earth to reach the Moon. Players will then be asked if they would like to play another round or exit the game.
